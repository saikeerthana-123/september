Word of the day - Day 67

numinous

Numinous describes things having a mysterious or spiritual quality.

UsageI was filled with a numinous sensation when, in the dark of night, a shooting star flashed across the sky. The tourists were overcome by the numinous atmosphere of the catacombs.
