Word of the day - Day 73

Sonder

The feeling when you realize that everyone you see, everyone who passes by has their own complex life.

UsageI had a sonder, a realization that the random girl sitting next to me inside of Starbucks might have a fantastic life or she might be dealing with a very ill family member
